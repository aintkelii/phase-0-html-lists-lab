Grilled cheese ingridients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack

Recipe for making Grilled cheese

  1. Spread butter on braed and frying pan

  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry

  3. Add cheese on top of braed

  4. Cover with second slice of bread

  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
